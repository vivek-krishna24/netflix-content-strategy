# netflix-content-strategy
**Netflix Content Strategy Analysis**
About This Project
This project analyzes Netflix's content strategy using Python. We look at how Netflix creates, releases, and distributes movies and TV shows to understand what makes their platform successful.
# What We Analyze

Content Types: Movies vs TV Shows
Release Dates: When content is released and timing patterns
Languages: Which languages have the most content
Genres: Popular categories like Drama, Comedy, Documentaries
Viewership Hours: How much people watch different types of content
Countries: Where content comes from

# Key Findings

Shows vs Movies: TV shows get more total watch hours than movies on Netflix
Language: English content dominates, but Korean and other languages also have good viewership
Popular Genres: Documentaries, Stand-Up Comedy, Dramas, and International Movies are top categories
Content Growth: Netflix significantly increased content between 2014-2020
